New features on Apple’s new iPhone 3G S help users of Cisco WebEx meetings

Alex Hadden Boyd - June 17, 2009 - 7 Comments

The new Apple iPhone 3G S on the new 3.0 operating system has many features that will be helpful to WebEx users on the iPhone">WebEx users on the iPhone. Here are my top three new features:Spotlight Search for WebEx meeting invitationYou can now quickly search and find that WebEx invitation whether it be in your email or in your calendar to simply join the meeting on your iPhone. Cut and pasteWhen you want to invite other people to a WebEx meeting, you now have option to simply copy and paste the WebEx meeting URL into a text. The recipient of the text simply needs to click on the url to join the WebEx meeting. Active URL link in calendarMy favorite—being able click on the WebEx meeting URL in the Calendar to join a WebEx meeting. The easiest way to schedule WebEx meeting is from the Outlook calendar or Lotus Notes using a plug in called WebEx Productivity Tools. Attendees will receive the meeting invitation in their calendar with the WebEx meeting info. To join the meeting, you can simply go to your calendar and tap on the meeting join link in the calendar invitation to join the meeting.With just these three features, iPhone users have more ways to quickly join WebEx meetings. But of course, we aren’t stopping there. The Cisco WebEx team is actively looking at adding additional features that leverage the iPhone 3.0 platform. But you’ll have to wait to hear more about those features. :-)by Alex Hadden Boyd, director of marketing, Cisco Collaboration Software Group
