New iPad App Helps Partners Grow Business

Anna Sui - September 13, 2011 - 2 Comments

With more than Apples-App-Store-Downloads-Top-10-Billion.html" target="_blank">10 billion Apple App Store downloads as of early 2011, there is literally an app for just about everything you can imagine—from carbon footprint calculators to addicting games like Angry Birds to apps that remember where you parked your car.

But did you know that there are a number of apps available to help you do more while you’re on the road, get partner news, test Internet speed, and even reboot a server from 30,000 feet? And in our newest app, the Cisco Small Business Solution Builder for the iPad, we’ll arm you with an arsenal of sales tools.

A free download, the Cisco Small Business Solution Builder app is now available to help grow your businesses. By answering a series of questions on the app, you’ll be provided with Cisco solutions and products to solve your customer’s business needs.

Here are some highlights of what partners can use the app to do:

  • Create custom designs from scratch or modify existing Cisco templates, with only a few taps
  • Match a customer’s needs with preconfigured and pretested Cisco SMART Designs
  • Browse a broad portfolio of Cisco products, and compare features and benefits
  • Get real-time pricing and availability from distributors on Velocity products using an integrated Fast Track 2 catalog
  • Present customer videos that make the business case for Cisco products
  • Retrieve saved solutions and customer information through email, enabling easy quote-building back at the office

And the app stays current with regular updates from Cisco so you can be sure that you are presenting the latest solutions and products to your customers at all times.

Want to find out how you can get this powerful new app?
Getting access to this app is easy and free! Simply search for the Cisco Small Business Solution Builder iPad app in the iTunes App Store, install, and voilà! You’re now ready to start growing your business.
